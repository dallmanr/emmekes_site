What is Project Euler?

Project Euler is a series of challenging mathematical/computer programming problems that will require more than just mathematical insights to solve. Although mathematics will help you arrive at elegant and efficient methods, the use of a computer and programming skills will be required to solve most problems.

Daily Programmer

Community inspired projects created weekly of various levels of difficulty. Monday easy challenges, Wednesday intermediate and Friday hard. These vary a lot on a weekly basis but I have found they have covered a lot of different aspects of programming and been quite enjoyable to boot.

Coding bat

Codingbat provides lots of exercises for Java and Python along with help videos for doing the exercises. Not quite as indepth as other resources but good for jumping in and out of.

Khan Academy

Probably one of the best known resources not just for programming but learning a whole range of different topics. This is a one stop shop for learning just about anything and comes highly recommended.
